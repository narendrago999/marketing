Marketing Plan Hub

Your guide to earning through social media platforms

LinkedIn

  • Create professional content related to your industry.
  • Engage in niche groups and grow your network.
  • Promote affiliate products or services via articles.
  • Offer consultation services or resume writing.
Ad Cost: $6–$9 per 1,000 impressions (CPM)
Potential Earnings: $100–$300/month via consulting, affiliate commissions & services if targeted correctly.

YouTube

  • Start a channel around a niche you love (tech, education, beauty).
  • Monetize via YouTube Partner Program (ads).
  • Use affiliate links in descriptions.
  • Partner with brands for sponsorships.
  • Sell your own digital products or courses.
Ad Cost: $0.10–$0.30 per view
Potential Earnings: $3–$5 per 1,000 views via ads; higher with affiliates and sponsorships.

Instagram

  • Grow a niche-based audience (fitness, fashion, food).
  • Work with brands for sponsored posts.
  • Promote affiliate links through stories and bio.
  • Launch and sell your own merchandise.
  • Offer shoutouts or promotions for other accounts.
Ad Cost: $0.20–$2 per click
Potential Earnings: $100–$500 per sponsored post with 10k+ followers; $50–$200/month via affiliate links.

Facebook

  • Create niche-specific pages and groups.
  • Monetize with Facebook Ads and brand deals.
  • Drive traffic to external blogs or YouTube.
  • Use Facebook Marketplace to sell products.
Ad Cost: $0.50–$2 per click
Potential Earnings: $100–$400/month through group promotions, external sales, and ads.

TikTok

  • Create viral and trending short videos.
  • Join TikTok Creator Fund once eligible.
  • Get brand deals and shoutouts.
  • Promote affiliate products with link-in-bio tools.
Ad Cost: $10 per 1,000 views (CPM)
Potential Earnings: $0.02–$0.04 per 1,000 views from Creator Fund; $100–$1000/month from sponsorships and affiliate marketing.

Twitter (X)

  • Engage in trending topics with niche authority.
  • Sell digital products or newsletters.
  • Monetize through sponsorships or Super Follows (if eligible).
Ad Cost: $0.50–$1.50 per engagement
Potential Earnings: $50–$300/month via digital sales, sponsorships, and Super Followers.

Blogging

  • Choose a niche and write SEO-rich content.
  • Earn via Google AdSense, affiliate marketing, and sponsored posts.
  • Grow email list and market digital products.
Ad Cost: $0.10–$0.50 per click (Google Ads)
Potential Earnings: $100–$1000/month depending on traffic, affiliate conversions, and sponsored posts.
